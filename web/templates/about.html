ABOUT

What is Foodie?

Foodie is a simple to use utility app for when you are stuck with foreign or super fancy menus. If there are any items you don't understand the description for, just open up Foodie, take a picture of the menu, and Foodie will translate the description into the language of your choice, while simplifying really long, complicated cooking terms.

What Inspired Foodie

The idea of Foodie was concieved when a young Owen Anderson couldn't understand anything on the menu of a fancy foreign restaurant. But let's be honest - who can blame him for not knowing what 'Abbacchio Cavolo' is?

Our Goal

To make eating out more convienent and less confusing for everyone! Good food should not = more stress!
